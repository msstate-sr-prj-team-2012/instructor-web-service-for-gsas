General
Getting Started
From the homepage, start by selecting a golfer either from the drop-down list or by manually entering the member’s id or email.
Next, in the rounds tab check up to 10 rounds whose data you would like to evaluate. If the round you want is not displayed, click view more for more available rounds. Click submit when done.
Change Golfer
At any point or location on the site, the current golfer can be changed by hovering the cursor over “Change Golfer?” on the far right.
The golfer selection methods will appear when the cursor is over the text. Select a new golfer either from the drop-down list or by manually entering the member’s id or email.
Change Listing Order of Tables
To change the order of any table, click on the tab of the column/field you wish to reorganize the table by.


Scores
View Holes and Shots
To view all the holes of a round, click the desired round under the Rounds table. To view all the shots of a hole, click the desired hole under the Holes table.


Maps
Change Rounds
To view the data from a different round, click on the desired round number listed along the left hand side of the map.
Change Holes
To view the data from a different hole, click on the desired hole number lined along the bottom of the map.
Toggle Map View
To toggle between the satellite view and the drawing view of the map, click on the desired view lined along the top of the map.
View Club and Distance Detail
Hover the mouse over the a point along the vector to view the club used and the distance at that point on the map.


Distance Graph
View Data by Club Type
To view the data of a particular club, select the corresponding tab above the graph.
View Data by Round
To view the data of a particular round, select the corresponding tab along the left side of the graph.
Isolate Data
To isolate data on the graph, an item can be toggled on and off by selecting the symbol of an item in the legend along the bottom of the graph.
View Distance
To view the distance hit by a particular club on the graph, hover the mouse over the item.

Admin
Edit Golfer
Click on the Admin link at the bottom of any page. In the new opened tab, select a golfer or enter the golfer’s id. Then click Edit Golfer. After changes have been made, click Save Changes.
Delete Golfer
Click on the Admin link at the bottom of any page. In the new opened tab, select a golfer or enter the golfer’s id. Then click Delete Golfer. Click Ok to confirm deletion of the selected Golfer.
Delete Rounds
Click on the Admin link at the bottom of any page. In the new opened tab, select a golfer or enter the golfer’s id. Then click Delete Rounds. Then select the rounds to delete and then click Delete.
